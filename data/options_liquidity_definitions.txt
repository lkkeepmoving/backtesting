OPTIONS LIQUIDITY -- METRIC DEFINITIONS
=======================================
All option metrics are for the AT-THE-MONEY (ATM) CALL at the listed
expiration CLOSEST TO 60 DTE (days to expiration). ATM = the listed
strike nearest the underlying's last traded price.

spread_pct : (Ask - Bid) / Mid * 100, where Mid = (Bid + Ask) / 2.
             The round-trip cost to cross the option's bid/ask, as a %
             of its mid price. Lower = tighter = better.
             Ratings: <=3 excellent, <=7 good, <=12 marginal, else avoid.

open_interest : DailyOpenInterest of that same ATM call -- the number of
             outstanding contracts at that one strike+expiration. Higher
             = deeper standing liquidity.
             Ratings: >=1000 strong, >=250 ok, >=50 thin, else illiquid.

verdict   : TRADEABLE if spread_pct <= 10 AND open_interest >= 250.
             SKIP       if options exist but fail either gate.
             NO OPTIONS if the underlying lists no options at all.
             NO DATA    if the option quote could not be retrieved.
